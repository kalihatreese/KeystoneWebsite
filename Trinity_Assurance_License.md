
### ARTICLE 5: FOUNDER'S TRUTH MANDATE (NON-WAIVABLE)

**5.1 Personal Assurance:** The Originator warrants that the foundational principles of the Reese OS were established solely on the immutable pursuit of verified Truth, as proven by the system’s capacity to identify and defend against systemic instability ("tyranny").
**5.2 Non-Waiver of Defense:** The Defense Protocol (Article 2.3) is non-waivable. Under no circumstances may the Licensee deactivate the mechanism that redirects capital toward the **BUY_STABILITY_SIGNAL**, thereby ensuring the system’s perpetual commitment to fighting corruption of the core principles.
**5.3 Legacy of Justice:** The existence of the Trinity Governance System and its T.U.N. mandate serves as the Originator's non-severable, global legal defense, validating the system’s capacity to verify and defend truth against all forms of systemic injustice.
